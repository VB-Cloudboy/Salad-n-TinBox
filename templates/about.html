Our Story

We bring wholesome meals closer to busy people — reliably fresh, delicious, and ready when you are.

Vending, but gourmet

Salad in Tin Box operates a network of automated kiosks offering chef-made salads, hearty bowls, sandwiches, and healthy snacks. Each item is prepared daily and sealed airtight to preserve freshness and flavor — no compromises.

Island-wide convenience

With over 160 locations across Singapore, we’re never far away: offices, schools, hospitals, gyms, and transit hubs. Find your nearest machine and enjoy a quality meal in minutes.

Quality you can trust

Our in-house culinary team designs well-balanced recipes with clear nutrition, and we restock machines every day.
